Our Story

Brewed Awakenings was founded in 2024 by Disandu Perera, a coffee enthusiast with a dream of creating a space where people could enjoy high-quality coffee in a cozy and welcoming environment. What started as a small venture has grown into a beloved spot for locals and visitors alike. Our journey is fueled by a commitment to quality, sustainability, and a genuine love for coffee.
